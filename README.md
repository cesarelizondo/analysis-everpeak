## 🔍 Key Insights

After cleaning and analyzing the dataset, several relevant findings were identified:

- Missing values were concentrated in customer location fields, requiring targeted imputation strategies.
- Sentinel values (e.g., -999) were successfully detected and replaced before analysis.
- Mean and median comparisons revealed the presence of outliers affecting purchase metrics.
- IQR and Z-Score methods produced complementary results for anomaly detection.
- Customer purchase behavior varies significantly by payment method and product category.
- Rule-based segmentation allows customers to be classified according to purchase volume and age.

## 💡 Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following recommendations could improve decision-making:

- Improve data validation to prevent invalid dates and sentinel values.
- Monitor high-value transactions detected as statistical outliers.
- Use customer segmentation to personalize marketing campaigns.
- Analyze purchasing behavior by payment method to optimize promotional strategies.
- Prioritize data quality controls before building predictive models.
